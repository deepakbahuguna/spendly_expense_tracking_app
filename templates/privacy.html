Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you log expenses, we store the amount, category, date, and any description you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Spendly service to you — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and powering filters. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the features you use.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and accessible only to your account. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts for typography. No personal or financial data is shared with any third party. We do not integrate with analytics providers, advertising networks, or external data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about how your data is handled, or wish to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the contact information available on our platform. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
